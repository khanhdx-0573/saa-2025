## Why It Matters

Peer recognition drives engagement and reinforces culture: Send Kudos gives Sunners a lightweight way to say thank-you to a colleague, and ties every note to one of the company's core-value hashtags (e.g. "BE PROFESSIONAL", "GO FAST", "WASSHOI"). Beyond the individual moment of recognition, the hashtag tagging creates a signal of which values are actively being lived day to day.

## Who Uses It

Any authenticated employee ("Sunner") of the app — as a sender composing and submitting a kudos, and as a recipient (or as a mentioned colleague) named within one. No special role or permission beyond being logged in is required to send or receive.

## What They Do

The sender opens the "Viết Kudo" modal (from the `/kudos` page), searches for and picks exactly one recipient, writes a short rich-text thank-you note (optionally mentioning another colleague inline with "@name"), tags the note with 1 to 5 hashtags chosen from the managed list, optionally attaches up to 5 images, optionally toggles "send anonymously" (entering a custom display name instead of their real identity), then submits. Submit is disabled until recipient, content, and hashtag selection are all valid; Cancel discards everything without confirmation.
